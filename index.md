## 🚀 Getting Started
Welcome to rating-sync! This application is a convenient Emby plugin that lets you sync IMDb community and Rotten Tomatoes critic ratings. It works with smart scanning, rate limits, and keeps a scan history. 

Follow these simple steps to get started.

## 💾 System Requirements
- Operating System: Windows, macOS, or Linux
- Emby Server: Version 4.0 or later
- Network: Internet connection for fetching ratings

## 📋 Features
- **Sync Ratings:** Easily sync ratings from IMDb and Rotten Tomatoes.
- **Smart Scanning:** Automatically scan your media library for updates.
- **Rate Limits:** Manage the frequency of your rating syncs to avoid overloading service requests.
- **Scan History:** Keep track of your past scans for easy reference.
